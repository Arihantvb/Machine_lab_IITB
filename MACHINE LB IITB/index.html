About us

The Electrical Machines Laboratory is an important part of the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Bombay. It serves as a lively learning space for students studying both undergraduate and postgraduate programs. . These practical activities help students understand the basic principles and real-world applications of electrical machines. They get hands-on experience working with transformers, generators, power electronics, motor drives, and more.
